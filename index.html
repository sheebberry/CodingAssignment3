Fresh Persian Salad Recipe

Salad Shirazi

Recipe by: Sheeba Soliamani

Ingredients

  • 4 Persian cucumbers
  • 1 whole sweet onion
  • 3 tomatoes on the vine
  • 4 tablespoons Dried mint
  • 1 teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 teaspoon of Pepper
  • 2 fresh limes
  • 1/4 cup of Olive oil

Equipment

  • Bowl
  • Cutting board
  • Knife

Directions

  1. Cut your onion in half, and then dice both halves into small bits.
  2. Wash and cut both ends off the cucumbers. Then from the top cut three lines spaced out down, then three lines across going all the way down before dicing the cucumbers back into small bits.
  3. Wash and dice your tomatoes into how big or small you'd like them to be.
  4. Once all your vegetables are cut, place them in a bowl.
  5. Set the bowl aside and grab your two limes. Roll them on the cutting board to loosen up the juices before cutting the limes in half.
  6. Squeeze two fresh limes into the bowl that has your vegetables.
  7. Add your salt, olive oil, and dried mint. Taste as you go.
  8. Top it all off with pepper before enjoying!
